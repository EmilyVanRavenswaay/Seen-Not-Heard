Young people across the world have been advocating for change, but more often than not they are met with backlash from those who are older. These older members of society often state that the youths are too young to be commentating on or trying to change what they see as problems. My project is trying to draw this issue into the light. To show the horrible things people are saying about children who are just trying to stand up for what they believe.

My graphic novel is divided into 6 part segments portraying an activist. Each set of 6 pages introduces a child activist, what they stand for, and provides segments of speeches they have given. These pages are filled with backlash; comments someone has made in attempts to lessen the worth of the child or stop them in their tracks. I hope to highlight the work the activists have done while drawing light to the almost childish reaction of adults.
